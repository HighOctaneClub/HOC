DRIVEN BY PASSION, POWERED BY PURPOSE

Established in 2024 by the students of DPS Dwarka, the High Octane Club (HOC) was founded with a clear vision to harness a shared enthusiasm for automobiles and transform it into a platform for innovation, collaboration, and applied creativity.

What began as a passion project among a few dedicated individuals has since evolved into one of the school's most dynamic and respected student-led communities.

EVOLVING ENGAGEMENT

HOC has consistently redefined student engagement in automotive sciences, from hands-on custom builds and engineering challenges to strategic auction-style competitions that blend negotiation and analysis.

A PLATFORM FOR EXCELLENCE

The club serves as a platform for experiential learning, technical excellence, and forward-thinking exploration in the world of automobiles.

OUR MISSION

To educate, inspire, and empower students through immersive learning experiences, technical workshops, and collaborative initiatives that reflect the rapidly evolving landscape of automotive innovation.

At HOC, curiosity meets engineering discipline, and passion is driven by purpose. For students who are eager to explore the intersection of technology, performance, and design, the High Octane Club is the starting point of a journey defined by precision, ambition, and relentless momentum.
